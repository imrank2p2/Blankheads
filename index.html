About

Blankheads are mysterious digital beings born without predefined identities. Their blank expressions represent limitless possibilities, allowing every collector to imagine a unique personality, story, and purpose behind each character. Designed in a bold 3D style with vibrant colors and distinctive details, every Blankhead is handcrafted to feel familiar yet completely unique. No two characters share the same combination of traits, making each one a collectible digital identity. Blankheads is more than an NFT collection. It is the beginning of a growing universe where creativity, individuality, and community come together to shape the future of every Blankhead.
